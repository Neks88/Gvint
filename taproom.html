TAPROOM

Beers can be tasted at the breweries taproom or in the garden. From the taproom and garden you can see the brewery’s production facility, which creates a direct sense of connection between the production and the consumption of beer. The taproom allows you to purchase large 2 liter bottles of Gvint beer, six packs, as well as products with our logo. People are more natural, more relaxed and more open in communication, while tasting beer. These are some of the reasons why the world of brewery is becoming more popular for different types of events. We can arrange Gvint tours – a visit to the breweries production facility , getting acquainted with beer production equipment and raw materials used for beer brewing, a conversation with the brew master. We can provide you with space and be at your service for business events, team buildings and similar activities.
